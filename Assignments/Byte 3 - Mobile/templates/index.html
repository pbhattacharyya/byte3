A Snapshot of Po's Activities on the Weekend of Feruary 24 & 25

I used the AWARE framework to use my smartphone's sensors to collect data about my location, movement, and activities. I then compiled the data and visualized some of the more interesting findings.

In the first visualization, you can see my locations in the Pittsburgh area over the course of the weekend (Saturday and Sunday). In the second visualization, you can track how I moved from one area to another over the course of each day, and what mode of transport (walking, biking, driving) I used. In the third visualization, you can see how often I dropped my phone (answer: twice, once on each day).

You can interact with the first and second visualizations to under how common each mode of movement was over the course of the weekend.
